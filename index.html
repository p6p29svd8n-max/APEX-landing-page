What You Get

Everything in one place.
Nothing left out.

AI Equity Research Reports

On-demand, structured investment reports generated in seconds by Claude AI and live web research. Covers thesis, DCF, risk factors, catalysts, and a personalised investment fit assessment.

Portfolio Tracker with AI Analysis

Track all your positions with live prices, P&L history, sector allocation charts, and AI-generated portfolio commentary. Sell transactions recorded with net proceeds and gain/loss history.

Interactive Stock View

Candlestick and line charts with full OHLCV tooltips across 1W–2Y ranges. Fundamentals panel, analyst price targets, recent news, short interest data, and insider transaction history.

Recession Radar

11-signal composite risk score across five regimes — from Expansion to Critical. Covers yield curve, HY spreads, VIX, Sahm Rule, LEI, and building permits. Capital allocation guidance per regime.

DCF Valuation Model

On-demand discounted cash flow analysis with WACC/terminal-growth sensitivity table, assumptions panel, and AI commentary explaining the key value drivers and risks for any ticker.

Newsletter Intelligence

Connect your email account to automatically ingest investment newsletters. AI extracts ticker mentions, macro themes, and key signals — turning unread email into structured investment intelligence.
